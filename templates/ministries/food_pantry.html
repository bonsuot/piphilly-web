Food Pantry Ministry

At PIWC Philly, we believe in sharing God’s love through service, compassion, and generosity. Our Food Pantry Ministry is dedicated to providing nutritious food and essential resources to individuals and families facing hardship. We are here to offer hope, encouragement, and support, ensuring that no one in our community goes hungry. Whether you are in need of assistance, looking to volunteer, or wanting to support this mission, we invite you to join us in making a difference. Together, we can be a beacon of hope and provision, reflecting Christ’s love through every meal shared. “For I was hungry and you gave me something to eat...” – Matthew 25:35

Food Pantry Ministry

Mission

To serve our community by providing nutritious food, compassionate support, and hope to individuals and families in need, reflecting God’s love through service and generosity.

Vision

A community where no one goes hungry, and all experience the love of Christ through access to food, resources, and support for a better tomorrow.
