Men's Ministry

The Pentecost Men’s Ministry (PEMEM) caters for the interest of all the men in the Church. The Men’s Ministry organizes seminars, workshops, lectures and symposia on all aspects of life including: Marriage enrichment, husbands’ responsibilities at home, child care and education; business management and techniques; leadership and responsibility in the Church and the State; current affairs at both national and international levels; the teaching of basic principles of domestic law and other specialized men-related ministries.

About Men's Ministry

Mission

A total Christian Man who is committed, spirit-filled and strong in Christ character to positively impact the family, the church and the community.

Vision

To become an effective and significant ministry to passionately reach Men for Christ in partnership with the church.
