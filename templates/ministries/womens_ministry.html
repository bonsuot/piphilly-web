Women's Ministry

Besides a core function of praying for the Church, the Women’s Ministry, which encompasses all women in the church, is responsible for giving employable and vocational skills, and training in home management to the women. The Women’s Ministry organizes retreats, seminars, workshops, lectures and symposia in all spheres of life, including (i) marriage enrichment, wives’ responsibilities at home, child welfare, care and education (ii) business management and techniques. It also carries out evangelism, counselling and sponsorship activities to help the needy in the church and in the society. Additionally, the Ministry ministers to single mothers and orphans, spinsters, commercial sex workers, lesbians, “street’ kids, widows, lady professionals and politicians, the aged, business women and HIV/AIDS victims. It undertakes hospital and media evangelism, contributes to the governments poverty alleviation program and performs any other specialized women-related ministry.
